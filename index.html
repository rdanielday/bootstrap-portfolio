Who I am

The first website I ever authored was dedicated to Dragonball Z and hosted on AngelFire. I was 11 years old. My skills have grown a lot since then, but the my fundamental excitement and passion about building for the web have stayed the same. The internet is an opportunity, and I want to reach out and grab it.

A better world is out there

I believe technology can be transformative. More importantly, technology must be designed and built with the intention of improving the world. I am passionately committed to designing software firmly rooted in humanism and community; I'm dedicated to promoting egalitarianism through excellent design, accessibility and usability.

I am available for hire in both contract and full-time positions. I also do freelance web development work. You can email me here, or send me a message on Github or Twitter!
